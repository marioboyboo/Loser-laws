The Standard Mandates

  • 01. The last player remaining is the ultimate loser.
  • 02. No player shall dispute the dealer's final word.
  • 03. If a "Loser Law" is broken, the offender must draw a penalty card.
  • 04. All scores must be visible to the group at all times.
  • 05. Silence is required during the "Executioner's Round."
